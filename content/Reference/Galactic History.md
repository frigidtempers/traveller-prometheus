> **Note:** Exact dates are unavailable for most recorded events. Events are listed in chronological order using surviving historical records.

## Origins

- Humanity evolves on Old Earth.
    
- Civilization develops.
    
- Industrialization begins.
    
- Spaceflight becomes possible.
    

---

## Expansion

- Practical cryptosleep technology enables interstellar travel.
    
- Humanity begins colonizing nearby star systems using relativistic spacecraft.
    
- Independent colonies are established throughout the Orion Arm.
    
- Continued expansion results in worlds separated by decades or centuries of travel.
    

---

## Colonization

- Planetary civilizations develop independently.
    
- Glitterworlds become centers of advanced science, medicine, and manufacturing.
    
- Urbworlds and midworlds expand through continued settlement and industrial growth.
    
- Distant frontier colonies become isolated rimworlds.
    

---

## Genetic Engineering

- Human genetic modification becomes commonplace on technologically advanced worlds.
    
- Stable xenotypes are developed for specialized environments and occupations.
    
- Numerous custom xenotypes are created throughout human space.
    

---

## Artificial Intelligence

- Artificial intelligences continue to increase in complexity.
    
- Some artificial intelligences eventually transcend human comprehension and become known as archotechs.
    
- Contact with archotechs remains uncommon and poorly understood.
    

---

## The Empire

- An interstellar empire emerges following the collapse of an earlier imperial civilization.
    
- Imperial authority spreads across numerous inhabited worlds.
    
- Noble houses establish regional control over Imperial territory.
    
- Psylinks become closely associated with Imperial governance.
    

---

## Present Day

- Humanity occupies worlds spanning every level of technological development.
    
- Most inhabited planets remain politically and economically independent.
    
- Interstellar travel continues using relativistic spacecraft and cryptosleep.
    
- Communication between worlds remains limited by the speed of light.
    
- New colonies continue to be founded while older settlements are abandoned, rediscovered, or forgotten.
    
- Traveller: Prometheus takes place during this period.
