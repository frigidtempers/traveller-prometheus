## Overview

Mechanoids are autonomous combat machines encountered throughout the galaxy. Although originally believed to be remnants of ancient interstellar wars, modern scholarship generally attributes their existence to Archotech influence, with many continuing to operate thousands of years after their original deployment.

Unlike conventional robots or synthetic xenotypes, Mechanoids are entirely autonomous. They do not negotiate, surrender, or retreat except as directed by their internal combat protocols. Dormant installations remain common throughout the Rim, where long-abandoned military complexes continue manufacturing replacement units despite the disappearance of those who originally constructed them.

On Prometheus, Mechanoids are most frequently encountered within ancient military facilities, derelict installations, and dormant mechanoid clusters buried beneath the surface.
