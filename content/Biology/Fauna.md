Prometheus supports a wide variety of indigenous and introduced animal species. Many were brought to the planet during successive waves of colonization, while others evolved naturally within the planet's ecosystems. The following entries provide a brief overview of the most commonly encountered fauna and their associated Traveller statistics. The game statistics below are taken directly from your existing supplement. 

---

## Boomalope

A large herbivore distinguished by volatile chemical sacs distributed throughout its body. When threatened or killed, these compounds ignite violently, making the Boomalope as dangerous in death as it is in life.

- **Hits:** 29–35
- **Speed:** 6
- **Skills:** Recon 0, Survival 1, Athletics (Endurance) 1
- **Attacks:** Hooves (1D), Bite (1D)
- **Traits:** Explosive (3D, 6m), Large (+1)

---

## Toxalope

Closely related to the Boomalope, the Toxalope replaces volatile compounds with corrosive toxic secretions. Their remains remain hazardous long after death, making disposal difficult.

- **Hits:** 29–35
- **Speed:** 6
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 1
- **Attacks:** Hooves (1D), Bite (1D)
- **Traits:** Toxic (3D, 6m), Large (+1)

---

## Boomrat

A small scavenger whose unstable internal chemistry causes it to explode when killed. Colonies of Boomrats are considered a significant hazard around settlements and industrial facilities.

- **Hits:** 1–2
- **Speed:** 2
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Athletics (Dexterity) 1
- **Attacks:** Bite (1)
- **Traits:** Explosive (2D, 3m), Small (-4)

---

## Waste Rat

Waste Rats thrive in polluted environments and abandoned industrial sites where few other animals can survive. They are notorious carriers of disease and should be avoided whenever possible.

- **Hits:** 1–2
- **Speed:** 2
- **Skills:** Survival 1
- **Attacks:** Bite (2)
- **Traits:** Diseased (6+, 1, Bite), Small (-4)

---

## Bog Hound

A fast-moving pack predator adapted to marshes and wetlands. Bog Hounds are fiercely territorial and will often pursue intruders well beyond the boundaries of their hunting grounds.

- **Hits:** 8–13
- **Speed:** 10
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Athletics (Dexterity) 2, Recon 0, Melee (Natural) 2
- **Attacks:** Claws, Bite (2D)
- **Traits:** Ornery, Tough, Small (-1)

---

## Megasloth

One of the largest naturally occurring herbivores found on Prometheus, the Megasloth is slow moving and generally docile. Despite its temperament, it is capable of inflicting devastating injuries when threatened.

- **Hits:** 50–70
- **Speed:** 6
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Claws (4D)
- **Traits:** Armour (+2), Slow Metabolism (-2), Large (+3)

---

## Muffalo

Domesticated across much of human civilization, Muffalo are prized for their strength, wool, and reliability as pack animals. Wild herds remain common throughout the grasslands of Prometheus.

- **Hits:** 29–35
- **Speed:** 6
- **Skills:** Survival 2, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Hooves (1D), Ram (2D)
- **Traits:** Armour (+2), Heightened Senses, Large (+1)

---

## Thrumbo

Thrumbos are among the rarest and most formidable animals known. Revered for their intelligence and extraordinary resilience, encounters with a Thrumbo are uncommon but rarely forgotten.

- **Hits:** 91–125
- **Speed:** 8
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 3, Melee (Natural) 1
- **Attacks:** Hooves (1D), Horn (4D)
- **Traits:** Armour (+4), Clever, Large (+4)

---

## Lava Snail

Found in volcanic regions and geothermal fields, Lava Snails possess thick mineralized shells capable of withstanding extreme temperatures. Their slow movement belies a surprising resilience.

- **Hits:** 14–28
- **Speed:** 2
- **Skills:** Survival 3, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 1
- **Attacks:** Slick Bite (1D)
- **Traits:** Slow Metabolism (-1), Large (+1)

---

## Mastodon

An enormous herbivore whose tusks and sheer mass make it one of Prometheus' most dangerous megafauna. Although generally peaceful, a charging Mastodon is capable of destroying lightly fortified structures.

- **Hits:** 71–90
- **Speed:** 6
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Melee (Natural) 2, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Hooves (1D), Tusk Gore (3D)
- **Traits:** Armour (+3), Tough, Large (+4)

---

## Alphabeaver

Despite their modest size, Alphabeavers can rapidly strip entire forests bare. Unchecked populations have caused significant ecological and economic damage on numerous colony worlds.

- **Hits:** 14–28
- **Speed:** 4
- **Skills:** Survival 2, Athletics (Strength) 1, Athletics (Endurance) 1
- **Attacks:** Bite (1D), Scratch (D3)
- **Traits:** Tough, Amphibious, Small (-1)

---

## Greatwolf

A highly social pack predator occupying the role of apex hunter across much of Prometheus' temperate wilderness. Greatwolves rarely attack settlements unless food becomes scarce.

- **Hits:** 29–35
- **Speed:** 8
- **Skills:** Survival 0, Recon 1, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Bite (2D)
- **Traits:** Tough, Ornery
