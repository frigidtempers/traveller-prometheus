## Overview

The **Saurid** is a biological xenotype engineered for survival in dense jungles, swamps, and tropical environments. Combining reptilian physiology with exceptional agility and environmental adaptability, Saurids excel as hunters, scouts, and wilderness survivalists.

Their distinctive scales, tails, and slit pupils make them among the most visually recognizable xenotypes.

---

## Biology

Saurids possess scaled skin, enhanced balance, rapid reflexes, and exceptional climbing ability. Their physiology performs most efficiently in warm climates, while prolonged exposure to cold environments can significantly impair physical performance.

Excellent spatial awareness and natural camouflage make Saurids highly effective in dense vegetation and uneven terrain.

---

## Society

Many Saurid communities maintain traditions centered around hunting, exploration, and environmental stewardship.

Although comfortable within modern civilization, Saurids are more commonly associated with frontier settlements where their environmental adaptations provide a distinct advantage.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Saurids are most frequently encountered within Prometheus' jungles, wetlands, and heavily forested regions.

Their expertise as trackers and hunters makes them valuable members of many frontier settlements.
