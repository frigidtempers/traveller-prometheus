## Overview

The **Starjack** is a biological xenotype engineered for prolonged habitation aboard spacecraft and orbital installations. Adapted to confined environments, extended microgravity exposure, and hazardous industrial conditions, Starjacks have become synonymous with humanity's interstellar infrastructure.

Although originally developed for orbital labor, Starjacks now occupy nearly every profession associated with spaceflight.

---

## Biology

Starjacks possess dense musculature, enhanced cardiovascular efficiency, and improved resistance to radiation and decompression-related trauma.

Generations of genetic refinement have also produced heightened spatial awareness and exceptional dexterity, qualities highly valued aboard spacecraft and orbital facilities.

---

## Society

Starjacks are commonly employed as engineers, pilots, technicians, station administrators, and spacecraft crew.

Many spend the majority of their lives away from planetary surfaces, developing strong professional traditions centered around cooperation, technical competence, and self-sufficiency.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Although relatively uncommon on the planetary surface, Starjacks are regularly encountered aboard orbital stations and transport vessels servicing Prometheus.

Many choose to remain in orbit throughout their careers, visiting the surface only when operational requirements demand it.

## Traveller Traits

### Space Cowboy

+2 **EDU**, along with either:

- +1 **Engineer** and **Electronics**, **or**
- +1 **Mechanic** and **Astrogation**

-2 **STR**

### Suit of Lead

Starjacks possess the equivalent of a permanently integrated **TL12 Vacc Suit** beneath their skin (without protective DM bonuses).

This allows survival in vacuum for up to **6 hours**.

After six hours, they begin suffocating, taking cumulative physical damage until rescued or deceased.
