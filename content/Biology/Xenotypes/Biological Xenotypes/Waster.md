## Overview

The **Waster** is a biological xenotype engineered for survival within heavily polluted and chemically hazardous environments. Exceptional resistance to toxins, disease, and industrial contaminants allows Wasters to thrive where most humans cannot.

Although originally developed for hazardous labor and post-industrial recovery efforts, Wasters have established populations throughout frontier and industrial worlds alike.

---

## Biology

Wasters possess extensive resistance to environmental toxins and infectious disease, supported by an accelerated metabolic system capable of processing a wide variety of chemical compounds.

These adaptations require regular stimulant use to maintain optimal neurological function, making chemical dependency a defining characteristic of the xenotype.

---

## Society

Wasters are commonly employed in waste processing, chemical manufacturing, salvage operations, and environmental remediation.

Despite persistent social stigma, many Wasters have built prosperous communities centered around industries considered too dangerous for other populations.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Wasters are most commonly encountered within industrial settlements, abandoned facilities, and regions contaminated by previous human activity.

Their resistance to hazardous environments frequently places them among the first settlers willing to reclaim otherwise uninhabitable territory.

## Traveller Traits

### Toxic Genealogy

+2 **END**

Immune to **pollution**, **disease**, and **overdose** effects.

### Psychite Dependency

To maintain **Toxic Genealogy**, Wasters must consume **Psychite** once per day.

For each day without Psychite:

- Lose **1 END**
- Lose all immunities granted by **Toxic Genealogy**

These penalties accumulate until Psychite is consumed.
