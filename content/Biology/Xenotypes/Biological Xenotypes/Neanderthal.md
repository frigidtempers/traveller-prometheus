## Overview

The **Neanderthal** is a resurrected human lineage recreated through ancient genetic material recovered from Old Earth. Originally intended as an anthropological achievement, Neanderthals have since established stable populations throughout human civilization, particularly on frontier and low-technology worlds.

Possessing exceptional physical endurance and resilience, Neanderthals are well suited to harsh environments where practical skills outweigh technological sophistication.

---

## Biology

Neanderthals possess dense bone structures, powerful musculature, and remarkable resistance to injury. These adaptations provide exceptional physical performance but are accompanied by reduced aptitude for abstract reasoning and complex social interaction compared to most engineered xenotypes.

Their robust physiology allows them to thrive in environments where modern humans often struggle.

---

## Society

Neanderthals are most commonly encountered among tribal societies, frontier settlements, and isolated colonies where practical survival skills remain essential.

Although often underestimated by outsiders, Neanderthals possess rich oral traditions and tightly knit communities that emphasize cooperation, resilience, and respect for experience.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Neanderthals are primarily found among Prometheus' tribal populations and remote frontier settlements.

Many serve as hunters, trackers, laborers, and warriors whose knowledge of the wilderness has been passed between generations.
