## Overview

The **Impid** is a biological xenotype engineered for survival in hot, arid environments. Distinguished by their natural pyrophagic glands, heat tolerance, and exceptional agility, Impids are among the most recognizable xenotypes throughout human civilization.

Originally developed for colonization efforts on extreme desert worlds, Impids have since spread far beyond their place of origin. Their ability to thrive in climates considered inhospitable to most humans has made them valuable pioneers, scouts, caravaners, and survivalists across countless frontier worlds.

---

## Biology

Impids possess numerous adaptations for life in high-temperature environments. Their metabolism conserves water efficiently, while specialized glands allow them to expel a short-range jet of combustible chemicals capable of igniting on contact with the air.

Their lean physique favors speed and agility over raw strength. As a result, Impids generally exhibit lower muscle mass than baseliners but are capable of covering great distances with remarkable endurance.

Horns, elongated ears, tails, and brightly colored skin are common physical characteristics of the xenotype. Although these features have earned them numerous superstitions throughout history, they possess no greater significance than the fur of a Yttakin or the tusks of a Pigskin.

---

## Society

Impids are commonly associated with frontier settlements, caravan companies, and nomadic communities adapted to life in harsh climates. Their familiarity with deserts and other arid environments has made them highly sought after as guides, scouts, and wilderness experts.

Their striking appearance has historically led to misunderstanding among populations unfamiliar with the xenotype. While some cultures associate them with danger or destruction, others regard them as symbols of resilience and independence, particularly on worlds where survival depends upon enduring unforgiving environmental conditions.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Impids are uncommon on Prometheus but are most frequently encountered in the planet's hotter equatorial regions and badlands.

Many work as caravan guides, hunters, prospectors, and independent explorers, while others have integrated into settlements where their environmental adaptations remain highly valued.

## Traveller Traits

### Going the Distance

+1 to **DEX** and **+2 Movement Score**, but **-1 INT** and **-1 STR**.

### Hellfire Breath

All Impids can spew fire from their mouths as a **Significant Action**. The Impid projects a **10-foot cone** of flame. Targets within the cone must succeed on a **DEX Check** or suffer **2D damage** and catch **On Fire**.
