## Overview

The **Highmate** is a biological xenotype engineered to excel in interpersonal relationships, diplomacy, and companionship. Developed to provide emotional support, artistic expression, and refined social interaction, Highmates possess exceptional charisma and emotional intelligence, coupled with a genetic aversion to violence.

Originally commissioned by wealthy patrons on highly developed worlds, Highmates have since spread throughout human civilization. While many continue to serve as diplomats, entertainers, counselors, and companions, others have sought lives independent of the roles for which they were originally engineered.

---

## Biology

Highmates possess numerous genetic modifications intended to encourage trust, empathy, and social cohesion. Their appearance is typically regarded as exceptionally attractive by human standards, while their emotional awareness allows them to recognize and respond to the feelings of others with unusual accuracy.

As part of their original design, Highmates possess genetic violence inhibitors that make deliberate acts of violence psychologically distressing. Although physically no less capable than a baseliner, most Highmates experience significant difficulty participating in combat except under extreme circumstances.

---

## Society

Highmates are closely associated with diplomacy, hospitality, performance, counseling, and public relations. Their natural social aptitude allows them to excel wherever negotiation, mediation, or emotional intelligence are valued.

The legacy of their creation remains a source of controversy throughout human civilization. Some societies regard Highmates as symbols of refinement and culture, while others criticize the circumstances under which many were engineered and sold as commodities. As legal recognition of xenotype rights has expanded, increasing numbers of Highmates have pursued independent careers and lives beyond their original intended purpose.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Highmates are uncommon on Prometheus.

Most arrived alongside Imperial officials, Corporate House representatives, or wealthy private expeditions. Others have intentionally settled on the Rim seeking anonymity and personal freedom, finding opportunities in frontier settlements where their past carries little significance.

---

## Variants

### Lowmate

The **Lowmate** is a derivative xenotype developed from the Highmate genome to provide a less costly alternative to the original design. Intended for widespread commercial distribution, Lowmate xenogerms became particularly popular on densely populated urbworlds, where they were frequently offered through long-term employment contracts rather than direct purchase.

While retaining much of the Highmate's natural charisma and social aptitude, Lowmates were engineered without the violence inhibitors present in the original genome. This reduced production costs while allowing them to perform occupations where confrontation or self-defense could become necessary.

Lowmates are commonly employed throughout hospitality, entertainment, public relations, politics, and the service industry. Many begin their lives under restrictive contractual agreements, though successful performers, entrepreneurs, and public figures often purchase or negotiate their independence. As a result, Lowmates occupy every level of society, from overworked service employees to celebrated entertainers and influential political personalities.

Although genetically similar to Highmates, the two xenotypes have developed distinct cultural identities. Many Highmates view Lowmates as a practical adaptation of an otherwise refined genome, while many Lowmates reject comparisons altogether, preferring to be recognized for their own achievements rather than the circumstances of their creation.

## Highmate Traveller Traits

### Born Socialite

+1 to SOC, Persuade, Carouse, and Art.

### Submissive by Design

Must succeed a Difficult INT Check or receive -2 to all Combat Actions.
### Lowmate Traveller Traits

#### Siren's Call *(Replaces Born Socialite)*

+1 to Persuade, Carouse, and Art (Performance).

#### Imperfect Copy *(Replaces Submissive by Design)*

Created without genetic violence inhibitors, Lowmates may freely engage in combat without suffering the penalties associated with **Submissive by Design**.
