## Overview

The **Hussar** is a biological xenotype engineered exclusively for military service. Developed as genetically enhanced assault troops, Hussars combine exceptional physical performance with accelerated healing, heightened aggression, and unwavering battlefield discipline. Their genome is intentionally dependent upon regular doses of Go-Juice, ensuring peak combat effectiveness while simultaneously preventing long-term independence from their creators.

Although originally fielded by planetary militaries and private security forces, Hussars can now be found throughout human civilization as mercenaries, bodyguards, military advisors, and veterans seeking lives beyond the battlefield.

---

## Biology

Hussars possess extensive genetic modifications designed to maximize combat performance. Enhanced musculature, reinforced skeletal density, accelerated clotting, and rapid tissue regeneration allow them to survive injuries that would incapacitate most other xenotypes.

The most recognizable physical characteristics of a Hussar are their crimson eyes and subdued emotional expression. Their neurological modifications suppress fear, hesitation, and many outward emotional responses, producing the stoic demeanor commonly associated with the xenotype. This expressionless appearance has earned them the informal nickname of the **"Hussar Stare"** among civilian populations.

Central to the Hussar genome is an engineered dependency on **Go-Juice**. Without regular administration, physiological performance rapidly deteriorates as withdrawal symptoms begin to impair both physical coordination and cognitive function.

---

## Society

Few xenotypes are as closely associated with a single profession as the Hussar. Throughout human civilization, they are synonymous with military service.

Many Hussars spend the entirety of their lives serving governments, mercenary companies, noble households, or Corporate Houses before retiring into civilian life. Others never receive that opportunity, remaining contract soldiers until death.

Their reputation often precedes them. Civilians frequently perceive Hussars as intimidating, emotionally detached, or inherently violent, despite many possessing perfectly ordinary personalities outside of combat. These misconceptions, coupled with their dependence on Go-Juice, have contributed to the widespread treatment of Hussars as military assets rather than individuals.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Hussars are uncommon on Prometheus but are encountered wherever organized military forces operate.

Most serve within Imperial garrisons, Corporate House security forces, mercenary companies, or as independent contractors hired to protect valuable settlements and trade caravans. A smaller number have abandoned military service altogether, attempting to build ordinary lives despite the biological dependence that continues to define them.

## Variants

### Uhlan

The **Uhlan** is a modified Hussar lineage engineered by insurgent movements seeking to counter conventional Hussar formations. Rather than creating an entirely new xenotype, early developers altered captured Hussar xenogerms, replacing their dependence on Go-Juice with **Luciferium** while further enhancing their combat performance.

Compared to Hussars, Uhlans are physically stronger, more aggressive, and significantly less stable. Their reliance on Luciferium grants remarkable battlefield capabilities but creates an irreversible dependency. Unlike Go-Juice withdrawal, the absence of Luciferium results in catastrophic systemic failure that inevitably leads to death.

Uhlans rarely remain in military service for long. Many become elite mercenaries, private enforcers, or infamous warlords whose reputations are matched only by the logistical challenge of sustaining their addiction.

## Hussar Traveller Traits

### Perfect Soldier

+1 to all Physical Characteristics (**STR, DEX, END**), along with **+1** to either **Gun Combat** or **Melee** while under the effects of **Go-Juice**.

### Submission by Addiction

Without **Go-Juice** for two days, a Hussar must succeed on a **Difficult (10+) INT Check** or suffer **-2** to all Combat Actions.

## Uhlan Traveller Traits

### Juggernaut *(Replaces Perfect Soldier)*

+1 to all Physical Characteristics (**STR, DEX, END**), along with **+2** to either **Gun Combat** or **Melee** while under the effects of **Luciferium**, but suffer **-2 SOC**.

### Sympathy for the Devil *(Replaces Submission by Addiction)*

Without **Luciferium** for longer than one day, the body begins to shut down. Uhlans take cumulative damage to their Characteristics beginning with **END** until death (1 on the first day, 2 on the second, 3 on the third, and so on).
