## Overview

The **Pigskin** is a biological xenotype engineered for physical resilience and rapid organ regeneration. Originally developed to support large-scale medical and industrial organ production, Pigskins possess remarkable durability, robust digestive systems, and an unusually high tolerance for injury.

Following widespread ethical reforms surrounding their treatment, Pigskins gradually established independent communities throughout human civilization. Although many continue to face prejudice stemming from their origins, they have become a common sight on frontier worlds where resilience is valued above appearance.

---

## Biology

Pigskins possess dense musculature, reinforced skin, and highly redundant internal organs that allow them to survive injuries that would prove fatal to most humans. Their digestive systems are capable of processing nearly any organic material, allowing them to survive in environments where conventional food sources are scarce.

Their stocky build and shortened facial structure reduce fine motor precision and depth perception, making precision tasks more difficult than for other xenotypes.

---

## Society

Pigskins are commonly associated with frontier settlements, heavy industry, agriculture, demolition work, and military service. Their physical durability has made them highly sought after in occupations where injury is an accepted risk.

Many Pigskin communities place considerable value on self-reliance, family, and mutual support. Although discrimination persists in parts of human civilization, Pigskins have established prosperous communities whose identities are no longer defined solely by the circumstances of their creation.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Pigskins are well represented throughout Prometheus, particularly among independent settlements and frontier communities.

Their resilience has allowed them to flourish in environments where food, medicine, and infrastructure remain unreliable, making them valued members of many isolated settlements.

---

## Variants

### Boarskin

The **Boarskin** is a heavily modified Pigskin lineage originally developed to perform hazardous labor and security duties within industrial facilities. Possessing greater physical strength and aggression than standard Pigskins, Boarskins became symbols of resistance during early Pigskin liberation movements and remain closely associated with martial traditions.

Although comparatively uncommon, Boarskins are respected for their courage, physical endurance, and unwavering loyalty to those they consider family.

## Traveller Traits

### Thick Skin

+1 to **STR**, **END**, and **Explosives**, **-2 DEX**, and **-1 INT**.

### Iron Stomach

Can consume any organic material in place of food, including plants, raw meat, and humanoid flesh.
