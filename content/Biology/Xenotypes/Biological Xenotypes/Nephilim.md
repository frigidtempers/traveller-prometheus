## Overview

The **Nephilim** are a rare biological xenotype engineered to combine advanced combat capability with powerful psychically mediated archite enhancements. Designed to oppose threats beyond the capabilities of conventional military forces, Nephilim possess extraordinary physical abilities sustained through specialized compounds known as **Holilly** and **Grace**.

Their distinctive appearance and uncommon abilities have led many cultures to associate them with religious imagery, though no evidence suggests a supernatural origin.

---

## Biology

Nephilim possess extensive genetic modifications supported by archite technology embedded throughout their bodies. These microscopic machines enable capabilities that would otherwise be impossible, including enhanced strength, limited flight, and extraordinary regenerative potential.

Maintaining these abilities requires regular access to Holilly and Grace, substances that replenish the archites responsible for their more advanced physiological functions.

---

## Society

Nephilim are exceptionally uncommon throughout human civilization.

Many dedicate themselves to military service, humanitarian efforts, or the protection of isolated settlements, while others pursue lives of contemplation or leadership. Regardless of occupation, their rarity often results in exaggerated reputations that blur the line between historical fact and folklore.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Only a handful of confirmed Nephilim are known to reside on Prometheus.

Most are associated with Imperial expeditions, religious communities, or independent protectorates operating far from established settlements.

---

## Variants

### Seraphim

Nephilim specialized in healing and preservation through advanced archite manipulation.

### Dominion

Nephilim engineered to stabilize critical biological functions and reverse otherwise fatal trauma.

### Archangel

Nephilim adapted for battlefield leadership through powerful psychically resonant vocal augmentation.

---

## Traveller Traits

### Divine Nature

Nephilim gain access to **Divine Nature**, allowing them to use angelic abilities by expending **Charges** (maximum **3** daily). Charges are regained through the consumption of **Holilly** and **Grace**.

+1 **SOC**

### Angelic Fury

Spend **1 Charge** to gain **+1 Gun Combat** and **Melee**. Must succeed on a **Difficult INT Check** or enter **Angelic Rage**, attacking random targets (including allies) for **1D3 Rounds**.

### Heavenly Flight

Spend **1 Charge** to fly up to your **Movement Score** using feathered wings. Ending movement airborne requires spending another **Charge** or immediately falling.

---

## Seraphim Traveller Traits

### Lay on Hands *(Replaces Angelic Fury)*

Expend up to **3 Charges** to automatically pass Medic checks by transferring archites into an injured Traveller.

---

## Dominion Traveller Traits

### Redemption *(Replaces Angelic Fury)*

Once per day, expend all **Charges** to revive a Traveller who has been dead for no longer than **5 minutes**. The revived Traveller is **Unconscious**, and both the Traveller and the Nephilim become **Fatigued**. This ability is restored only after consuming **Grace**.

---

## Archangel Traveller Traits

### Holy Hymn *(Replaces Angelic Fury)*

Expend up to **3 Charges**. Allies within eyesight gain **DM+1** to all attack rolls until the end of combat.
