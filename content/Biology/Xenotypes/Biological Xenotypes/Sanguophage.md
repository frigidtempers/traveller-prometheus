## Overview

The **Sanguophage** is a rare biological xenotype distinguished by extensive archite modification. Possessing extraordinary regenerative capabilities, exceptional longevity, and a dependence upon human blood, Sanguophages have become the subject of countless myths throughout human civilization.

Although commonly associated with aristocratic dynasties and secluded estates, Sanguophages occupy every level of society. Their rarity and unusual physiology have contributed to a reputation that often blurs the line between historical fact and folklore.

---

## Biology

Sanguophages possess dense concentrations of archites that continuously repair cellular damage, dramatically extending lifespan and enabling recovery from injuries that would prove fatal to most humans.

This regenerative capability is sustained through the consumption of human blood, which provides the biological material required by their archites. Extended deprivation results in increasing physiological instability and eventual death.

Most Sanguophages display pale complexions, elongated canines, and heightened sensitivity to bright light, though physical appearance varies considerably between individuals.

---

## Society

Throughout history, Sanguophages have occupied positions ranging from secluded scholars and physicians to nobles, military commanders, and industrial magnates.

Their longevity often grants them access to generations of accumulated experience, while their dietary requirements frequently encourage isolation from broader society. Although misconceptions remain widespread, most Sanguophages integrate into human civilization through careful management of their condition.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Confirmed Sanguophages on Prometheus are exceptionally rare.

Most reside within Imperial territories or isolated estates, though scattered reports suggest independent individuals may also inhabit the frontier beyond established settlements.

## Variants

### Bruxa

An agile lineage emphasizing speed, stealth, and predatory instinct.

### Ekkimian

A physically imposing lineage engineered for overwhelming strength and battlefield resilience.

### Strigoi

A specialized lineage exhibiting heightened archite activity at the expense of increased blood dependency.

---

## Sanguophage Traveller Traits

### Vampiric

-1 to all Characteristics while in **Sunlight**, +1 to all Characteristics otherwise. Immune to disease.

Sanguophages gain no sustenance from conventional food or drugs and must instead feed upon **humanoid blood**.

---

## Bruxa Traveller Traits

### Embrace

Can remove the effects of **poison, disease, or pollution** from anyone whose blood they drink.

If feeding upon another **Sanguophage**, both the Bruxa and the target gain **+1 to all Characteristics** for **1D3 hours**.

### Sensitive Palette

Can only feed upon **humanoid blood**.

---

## Ekkimian Traveller Traits

### Corpse Feeder

May only feed upon **humanoid corpses** and cannot feed upon the living.

### Secretions

Once per day, produce a **10m Toxic Cloud** centered on yourself. Anyone inside, including allies, must succeed on a **Difficult END Check** or become **Poisoned**.

Alternatively, spit a corrosive secretion at a single target within **10m**, dealing **1D3 damage** that ignores armor.

---

## Strigoi Traveller Traits

### Monstrous Form

Gain **2 Charges** of Monstrous Form each day.

While active, gain **bat-like wings** capable of flight up to your **Movement Score**, along with **Claws** functioning as a **Natural Weapon** with the same statistics as a **Claw Glove**.

Charges are restored after feeding upon blood.
