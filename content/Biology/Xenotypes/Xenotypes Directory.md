# Xenotypes

> [!info] Orbital Communications Console Uplink

>
> **Archive Status:** Active
>
> **Source Reliability:** High

Xenotypes are distinct forms of humanity created through genetic engineering or artificial construction. While most xenotypes are biological descendants of the human genome, advances in robotics and artificial intelligence have also resulted in synthetic persons that occupy a comparable role within human civilization.

This archive classifies all recognized forms of humanity under the designation **Xenotype**, separating them into **Biological Xenotypes** and **Synthetic Xenotypes** for administrative purposes.
