## Overview

The **Mechanent** is a synthetic xenotype engineered as a purpose-built industrial worker. Designed long before the widespread adoption of modern Androids, Mechanents prioritize reliability, efficiency, and ease of maintenance over human appearance or behavioral complexity.

Although frequently mistaken for mechanoids by those unfamiliar with synthetic technology, Mechanents were designed to work alongside humans rather than replace them. Their modular construction and specialized Persona Cores allow them to perform demanding occupations with exceptional consistency, though at the cost of adaptability.

---

## Construction

Mechanents are constructed around durable mechanical frames powered by rechargeable energy cells and governed by specialized Persona Cores. Unlike Androids, they possess few synthetic biological systems, making repairs straightforward through conventional engineering rather than surgical intervention.

Each Mechanent is manufactured with software and hardware optimized for a particular occupational role. While this specialization greatly improves efficiency, it also limits their ability to adapt beyond their intended purpose without significant modification.

Their unmistakably mechanical appearance varies depending on manufacturer and intended function, though most retain a humanoid body plan to simplify the use of human equipment and infrastructure.

---

## Society

Mechanents are commonly employed throughout industry, manufacturing, logistics, construction, and hazardous environments where continuous operation is essential.

Unlike Androids, Mechanents are seldom regarded as individuals. Most remain the property of governments, corporations, or private owners and are viewed as sophisticated industrial equipment rather than autonomous citizens. Nevertheless, long-serving Mechanents occasionally develop distinct personalities through years of accumulated operational experience, blurring the distinction between machine and person.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Mechanents are encountered throughout Prometheus wherever heavy industry or advanced manufacturing exists.

Mining operations, orbital facilities, Corporate House installations, and major settlements frequently employ Mechanents to perform hazardous or repetitive work. Older models are particularly common among independent frontier settlements, where their modular construction allows them to remain operational decades after their original manufacturers ceased production.

## Traveller Traits

### Easily Modifiable *(Replaces Synthetic Construction)*

Once per day, choose **one** of the following modifications:

- **Advanced Subroutines:** +1 **INT** and **EDU**
- **Enhanced Plating:** +1 **STR** and **END**
- **Ultra-Light Materials:** +1 **DEX** and **Movement Score**
- **Translation Software:** +1 **SOC** and the ability to speak all known human languages.
- **Protocol Droid:** +1 to **Admin**, **Advocate**, and **Diplomat**.
- **Repair Subroutines:** +1 to **Engineer**, **Mechanic**, and **Electronics**.

### Battery Operated

Mechanents do not require food or water.

Instead, they must spend **8 hours** each day connected to an appropriate charging source. Failure to recharge causes the Mechanent to become **Fatigued** until sufficient power has been restored.

Mechanents are immune to the effects of aging.
