## Overview

The **Android** is a synthetic xenotype designed to replicate the appearance and behavior of a human while exceeding organic limitations. Powered by neutroamine-fueled synthetic organs and governed by a persona subcore, Androids are capable of independent reasoning, emotional simulation, and long-term autonomous operation.

Originally developed to perform hazardous industrial, scientific, and exploratory work, Androids have become commonplace throughout human civilization. Their outwardly human appearance allows them to integrate easily into society, though legal recognition of their personhood varies considerably between worlds.

---

## Construction

Unlike conventional robots or mechanoids, Androids are built around synthetic organs designed to emulate biological systems. Their artificial musculature, circulatory systems, and neural architecture allow them to function in a manner nearly indistinguishable from an organic human.

Although exceptionally resilient to disease and environmental hazards, Androids cannot naturally heal damaged components. Structural repairs require surgical replacement of damaged synthetic organs rather than conventional mechanical maintenance.

Android cognition is governed by a **Persona Subcore**, an advanced artificial intelligence capable of learning through experience while remaining fundamentally limited by its original programming.

---

## Society

Androids occupy nearly every profession throughout human civilization. Their endurance, precision, and immunity to biological illness make them particularly valuable in medicine, engineering, mining, industrial production, and deep-space exploration.

Attitudes toward Androids differ considerably between societies. Some worlds recognize them as legal persons possessing the same rights as biological humans, while others continue to classify them as manufactured property. These conflicting philosophies have become the subject of ongoing legal and ethical debate throughout inhabited space.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Androids are uncommon but well known on Prometheus.

Most are employed by Corporate Houses, orbital facilities, and technologically advanced settlements where their specialized capabilities justify the considerable expense of their manufacture and maintenance. Independent Androids are rare but not unknown, particularly among traders, explorers, and former corporate assets who have secured their own autonomy.

---

## Variants

### Awakened Android

An **Awakened Android** is an Android whose Persona Subcore has undergone spontaneous self-actualization following prolonged operation or extreme emotional stimulus. The precise mechanism responsible for this phenomenon remains poorly understood.

Unlike conventional Androids, Awakened Androids are no longer constrained by many of the behavioral limitations imposed during manufacture. They demonstrate accelerated learning, genuine emotional development, and remarkable adaptability beyond the capabilities of standard Persona Subcores.

Because the Awakening process cannot be intentionally reproduced, Awakened Androids remain exceptionally rare. Their existence continues to fuel debate regarding artificial consciousness, legal personhood, and whether true self-awareness can emerge from manufactured intelligence.

>[!info] Xenotype Note:
>Players seeking to play as an Awakened Android must choose to play as an Android and succeed on a DM 11+ INT check this can be done at any point during the course of their Career Terms however benefits are not gained until Awakening is achieved.

## Android Traveller Traits

### Synthetic Construction

Androids do not require food or water. Instead, they must consume at least one dose of **Neutroamine** each day.

Androids do not require sleep and are immune to the effects of aging.

+1 **END**, +1 **INT**

### Soulless

Androids cannot utilize **Psylink Neuroformers** or otherwise develop psychic abilities.

---

## Awakened Android Traveller Traits

### Awakening

Gain the benefits of **one additional Career Term** at character creation.

Having broken free from the limitations of their original programming, Awakened Androids learn and adapt through experience with remarkable speed.
