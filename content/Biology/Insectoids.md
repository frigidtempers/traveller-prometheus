## Overview

Insectoids are genetically engineered arthropods originally developed as biological weapons. Following the collapse of the original government that created them, numerous populations established feral breeding colonies throughout human space. On Prometheus, insectoid hives are primarily encountered beneath mountains, abandoned mining complexes, and other extensive subterranean environments.

Although individual castes vary considerably in size and behavior, all insectoids display highly territorial instincts and will aggressively defend their hive against perceived threats. Their durable exoskeletons and rapid reproductive cycles make established infestations extremely difficult to eradicate.

---

## Megaspider

The largest commonly encountered worker caste, Megaspiders serve as the primary defenders of the hive. Their thick exoskeleton and immense strength make them exceptionally dangerous in close quarters.

- **Hits:** 36–49
- **Speed:** 4
- **Skills:** Melee (Natural) 2, Recon 0, Athletics (Endurance) 3
- **Attacks:** Head Claw (3D)
- **Traits:** Armor (+6), Tough, Ornery, Large (+1)

---

## Megascarab

Small but heavily armored, Megascarabs function as scavengers and maintenance organisms within the hive. Individually they pose little threat, though large groups can quickly overwhelm isolated targets.

- **Hits:** 8–13
- **Speed:** 2
- **Skills:** Survival 0, Melee (Natural) 1, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Mandibles (1D)
- **Traits:** Armor (+1), Tough, Ornery, Small (-3)

---

## Spelopede

Spelopedes patrol the outer tunnels of the hive, responding rapidly to disturbances. Faster and more agile than Megaspiders, they frequently hunt in coordinated groups.

- **Hits:** 14–28
- **Speed:** 4
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Melee (Natural) 1, Athletics (Endurance) 2
- **Attacks:** Head Claw (1D)
- **Traits:** Armor (+1), Tough, Ornery, Small (-1)

---

## Locust

Locusts are winged insectoids capable of short-distance flight. They primarily function as scouts, spreading the hive's influence into surrounding cave systems while locating new food sources and suitable nesting sites.

- **Hits:** 6–7
- **Speed:** 4
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Recon 0, Athletics (Dexterity) 2
- **Attacks:** Mandibles (D3)
- **Traits:** Particulate, IR/UV Vision, Flyer (Slow), Small (-4)

---

## Hive Queen

The Hive Queen serves as the reproductive center of an insectoid colony. Vastly larger than any other caste, she rarely leaves the brood chamber, directing hive activity through pheromonal communication while continuously producing new insectoids.

The destruction of a Hive Queen often causes a colony to fragment, though surviving castes may continue defending the nest for extended periods before eventually dying off.

- **Hits:** 91–125
- **Speed:** 2
- **Skills:** Survival 1, Athletics (Endurance) 3
- **Attacks:** Head Claw (5D)
- **Traits:** Armor (+16), Tough, Ornery, Large (+4)
