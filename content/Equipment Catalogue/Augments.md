Augments encompass any technology permanently integrated into the body to restore, replace, or enhance biological function. From simple prosthetic limbs fitted after industrial accidents to impossibly sophisticated archotech implants, augmentation has become commonplace throughout the Known Worlds. While frontier settlers often rely upon salvaged prosthetics, glitterworld citizens may possess enhancements virtually indistinguishable from natural anatomy.

---

## Prosthetics

Designed to replace lost or damaged limbs and organs, prosthetics restore basic functionality but rarely equal the performance of healthy biological tissue. They are inexpensive, widely available, and commonly found throughout frontier settlements.

### Prosthetic Arm

*"Better than nothing."*

**TL:** 9

**Cost:** Cr2,500

**Effect:**
- Restores the use of a missing arm.
- STR and DEX checks made with this limb suffer **DM–1**.

---

### Prosthetic Leg

*"A practical solution for those who still have places to be."*

**TL:** 9

**Cost:** Cr2,500

**Effect:**
- Restores mobility.
- Movement is reduced by **25%** while using a prosthetic leg.

## Bionic Augments

Modern cybernetic replacements not only restore lost function but frequently exceed natural biological capability. Although expensive, they are common among soldiers, industrial workers, and wealthy citizens.

### Bionic Arm

*"Built stronger than the one you were born with."*

**TL:** 12

**Cost:** Cr15,000

**Effect:**
- Restores a missing arm.
- Grants **DM+1** to STR and DEX checks involving the arm.


---

### Bionic Eye

*"Never blink."*

**TL:** 12

**Cost:** Cr12,000

**Effect:**

### Bionic Legs
