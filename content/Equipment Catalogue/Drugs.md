Drugs and other substances are ubiquitous throughout human space. Some are essential medicines that prevent disease or enhance recovery, while others are recreational substances, military stimulants, or dangerous narcotics traded through legal and illicit markets alike. Although many drugs offer significant benefits, prolonged use often carries risks ranging from dependency to permanent physiological damage.

---
## Recreational Substances

Although generally considered safe when consumed responsibly, recreational substances remain among the most widely traded commodities throughout human space. Their legality varies from world to world, with frontier settlements often imposing few restrictions while more developed societies regulate their production and distribution through taxation and licensing.

---

### Beer

*"The first beverage besides water ever consumed by Mankind."*

**Cost:** Cr5

**Effects**

**Warm (1 Drink):**
Reduced pain threshold, **–1 DEX**, **+1 END**.

**Tipsy (2 Drinks):**
**–1 INT**, **–1 DEX**, **+1 CHA**, **+1 END**. Minor euphoria and reduced pain threshold.

**Drunk (4 Drinks):**
**–2 DEX**, **–2 INT**, **+2 CHA**, **+2 END**. Movement halved and risk of a hangover.

**Hammered (6 Drinks):**
**–3 DEX**, **–3 INT**, **–3 CHA**, **+3 END**, **–1 Initiative**, and **DM–2** on Mechanic, Electronics, and Gun Combat checks.

**Blackout (8 Drinks):**
Roll **Difficult (10+) END**. On a failure, fall unconscious for **1D hours**. On a success, the Referee temporarily assumes control as the character remembers only fragments of their actions.

**Cirrhosis**
Consuming **8 drinks** within **1D days** requires a **Difficult (10+) END** check. Failure causes a permanent **–1 END**.

**Tolerance**
Gain **+1 Tolerance** whenever consumed. At **10+**, roll for addiction. At **20+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **3 days** without use.

**Hangover**

- **Pounding:** Temporary **–2 INT**, vomiting for **1D hours**
- **Strong:** Temporary **–1 INT**, Routine (6+) END check for nausea
- **Mild:** Temporary headache
- **Nothing:** No lingering effects

**Addiction**
If consumed more than once every day, roll an **Average (8+) END** check.

**Withdrawal**
Roll an **Average (8+) END** check. Every failure temporarily reduces **END** and **INT** by **1**.

### Ambrosia

*"Soft, rare, tastes wonderful, and improves mood."*

**Cost:** Cr500

**Effect**
Temporary **+1 DEX** and **–1 END**.

**Crash**
No fatigue crash, but another dose provides no benefit until **8 hours** have passed.

**Tolerance**
Gain **+1 Tolerance** per use. At **2+**, roll **Average (8+) END** or become addicted. At **5+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **5 days** without use.

**Addiction**
If consumed more than once every **2 days**, roll an **Average (8+) END** check.

**Withdrawal**
Roll **Average (8+) END** once per day. Failure causes **1 temporary END** damage.

### Psychite Tea

*"Fragrant tea infused with psychite leaves provides a subtle euphoria."*

**Cost:** Cr200

**Effect**
For **1D hours**, gain **+1 END**, **+1 DEX**, **–1 INT**, ignore Fatigue caused by missed rest, and ignore pain.

**Crash**
Become **Fatigued** until receiving proper rest or succeeding on a **Routine (6+) Medic** check.

**Tolerance**
Gain **+2 Tolerance** per use. At **10+**, roll **Average (8+) END** to avoid addiction. At **20+**, addiction is automatic. Lose **1 Tolerance** every **7 days** without use.

**Addiction**
If consumed more than **4 times** within **24 hours**, roll **Average (8+) END** or suffer **–1 END** (kidney stress) and become addicted.

**Withdrawal**
Roll **Difficult (10+) END** daily. Failure causes **–1 SOC**, **–1 END**, **–1 DEX**, halved Movement, Fatigue, and Irritability.

### Smokeleaf

*"A fixture in many traditional low-industrial cultures."*

**Cost:** Cr50

**Effect**
For **2D hours**, gain **+1 END**, **–1 INT**, **–2 Initiative**, and Movement is halved. The user is considered **Fatigued** and enters a **Mellow** state.

**Mellow**
Ignore minor discomforts such as pain or distractions and gain **+1 CHA** during non-stressful social interactions.

**Crash**
Remain **Fatigued** until sleeping or receiving a successful **Routine (6+) Medic** check.

**Tolerance**
Gain **+1 Tolerance** per use. At **10+**, roll **Average (8+) END** to avoid addiction. At **15+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **7 days** without use.

**Addiction**
Using Smokeleaf more than once every **2 days** requires an **Average (8+) END** check.

**Withdrawal**
Roll **Average (8+) END** or **INT** daily. Failure causes **–1 INT**, **–1 DEX**, Fatigue, and halved Movement.

**Carcinoma Risk**
After one year of continual use, make a **Difficult (10+) END** check each month. Failure may result in chronic lung disease at the Referee's discretion.

## Narcotics

Narcotics are powerful psychoactive substances whose use is heavily regulated—or outright prohibited—throughout much of the Known Worlds. Their potency makes them valuable commodities for smugglers, criminal organizations, and desperate frontier settlements alike, though prolonged use frequently results in severe addiction, lasting physical harm, or irreversible psychological damage.

---

### Flake

*"Known for destroying lives, communities, and entire societies."*

**Cost:** Cr100

**Effect:** Gain **+1 INT** and **+1 DEX**. Become immune to fear and panic for **1D hours**, and always act first during Initiative. After the effect ends, the user becomes **Fatigued** until they sleep or receive medical treatment. Taking another dose while Fatigued immediately renders the user unconscious.

**Crash:** When the effects wear off, become **Fatigued** and make an **Average (8+) END** check. On a failure, suffer **–2 INT** and **–1 DEX** for **1D hours**.

**Tolerance:** Gain **+3 Tolerance** per use. At **6+**, roll for addiction. At **10+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **10 days**, or through a supervised detoxification program.

**Addiction:** Using Flake more than once within **24 hours** requires a **Difficult (10+) END** check. Failure results in addiction and a permanent **–1 SOC** or **–1 END** (player's choice).

**Withdrawal:** Make a **Very Difficult (12+) END** check once per day. On a failure, suffer **–2 INT**, **–1 DEX**, become **Fatigued**, and experience hallucinations at the Referee's discretion. After **three consecutive failures**, make an **Average (8+) END** check or permanently lose **1 INT**.

### Yayo

*"Chi Chi, get the yayo."*

**Cost:** Cr200

**Effect:** Gain **+2 INT**, **+1 DEX**, and **+1 Initiative** for **1D hours**. Ignore all Fatigue for the duration.

**Crash:** When the effects end, become **Fatigued** and make a **Routine (6+) END** check. On a failure, suffer **–1 INT** and **–1 DEX** for **1D hours**.

**Tolerance:** Gain **+2 Tolerance** per dose. At **6+**, make an **Average (8+) END** check after each use to avoid addiction. At **10+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **7 days** without use.

**Addiction:** Taking more than one dose within **24 hours** requires a **Difficult (10+) END** check. Failure results in addiction.

**Withdrawal:** Make a **Difficult (10+) END** check once per day. Each failure causes **–2 INT**, **–2 DEX**, **Fatigue**, irritability, and a **Boon** on all Mental skill checks. After **three failed checks**, permanently lose **1 INT** unless successfully treated.

**Recovery:** Withdrawal lasts **1D6 days**. Each successful **Routine (6+) Medic** check reduces the remaining recovery time by **1 day**.

### Psilocap

*"The forest is not only good to eat but good to think about."*

**Cost:** Cr300–600 (depending on legality and purity)

**Effect:** For **1D × 4 hours**, gain **+1 CHA** and **+1** to either **Art (Performance or Expression)** or **Science (Psionicology)**. Suffer **Banes** on all **INT-based skill checks** except the chosen Art or Science specialty. Users commonly experience vivid hallucinations throughout the duration.

**Special:** After one hour, make a **Routine (6+) INT** check. On a success, gain **Creative Surge**, granting **+1** to the next **INT-** or **CHA-based** check made within the next **24 hours**. On a failure, no additional effect occurs.

**Overdose:** Taking more than one dose within **24 hours** requires a **Difficult (10+) END** check. Failure results in catatonia for **1D × 4 hours** (the user is unconscious but stable). If this occurs twice within a **7-day** period, permanently lose **1 INT**.

**Crash:** No mechanical crash occurs, but the user becomes **Fatigued** and suffers **–1 Initiative** for **4 hours** after the effects end.

**Tolerance:** **+0** per use. Psilocaps do not build tolerance.

**Addiction:** Psilocaps are **not addictive**.}

## Combat Stimulants

Combat stimulants are engineered to enhance human performance in life-threatening situations. Originally developed for military personnel, emergency responders, and hazardous industrial workers, these drugs temporarily push the body beyond its natural limits at the cost of significant physical strain. While highly effective, repeated use frequently results in dependency, debilitating withdrawal, or permanent physiological damage.

---

### Go-Juice

*"Developed for space marines in the early Hyper-War."*

**Cost:** Cr750

**Effect:** For **1D hours**, gain **+2 DEX**, **+1 STR**, and **+1 Initiative**. Ignore pain and shock. Whenever you make an **Average (8+) END** check to resist Fatigue, roll with a **Boon**.

**Crash:** After the duration expires, become **Fatigued** and make a **Difficult (10+) END** check. On a failure, suffer **–2 DEX** and halve your Movement for **1D hours**.

**Tolerance:** Gain **+3 Tolerance** per use. At **10+**, make a **Difficult (10+) END** check to avoid addiction. At **15+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **10 days** without use.

**Addiction:** If used more than once every **48 hours**, make a **Difficult (10+) END** check. On a failure, become addicted.

**Withdrawal:** Make a **Difficult (10+) END** check once per day. On a failure, suffer **–2 STR**, **–2 DEX**, become **Fatigued**, and suffer **Banes** on **Melee** and **Gun Combat** checks.

After **three consecutive failed withdrawal checks**, permanently suffer **–1 DEX**.

**Special (Hussars):** While under the effects of Go-Juice, Hussars gain **+1** to either **Gun Combat** or **Melee**. Missing a dose for **2 or more days** triggers the **Submission by Addiction** trait.

### Wake-Up

*"...Most universities call high-achievers 'wake-ups' because of the association with the drug."*

**Cost:** Cr400

**Effect:** For **1D × 4 hours**, ignore all **Fatigue**, including Fatigue caused by missed rest. Gain **+1 DEX** and **+1 INT**, but suffer **–2** to all **CHA-based** checks.

**Crash:** When the effects end, become **Fatigued** and make an **Average (8+) END** check. On a failure, suffer **–1 INT**, are unable to sleep for **1D hours**, and suffer **Banes** on all Mental skill checks until you receive proper rest.

**Tolerance:** Gain **+2 Tolerance** per use. At **8+**, make an **Average (8+) END** check to avoid addiction. At **15+**, addiction is automatic. Tolerance decreases by **1** every **7 days** without use.

**Addiction:** If used more than once every **24 hours**, make a **Difficult (10+) END** check. On a failure, become addicted.

**Withdrawal:** Make a **Difficult (10+) END** check once per day. On a failure, suffer **–1 INT**, **–1 DEX**, become **Fatigued**, and become too jittery to benefit from natural rest that day.
