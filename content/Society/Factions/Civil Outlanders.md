## Overview

**Civil Outlanders** are independent industrial settlements that exist outside the direct authority of larger interstellar institutions. While their forms of government vary considerably, they are generally characterized by stable leadership, developed industry, organized commerce, and a willingness to engage in diplomacy.

Unlike the Corporate Houses, Civil Outlanders are not interstellar organizations. Each settlement governs only itself and whatever territory it can reasonably administer. Relations between neighboring communities range from close alliances to complete isolation.

Most travelers encounter Civil Outlanders as trading partners, refueling stops, or temporary places of refuge while crossing the frontier.

---

## Characteristics

Civil Outlander settlements generally share several common traits.

- Stable civilian governments
- Permanent industrial infrastructure
- Active trade with neighboring settlements
- Organized law enforcement
- Defensive military forces
- Open diplomacy with outsiders

Although willing to defend themselves, Civil Outlanders rarely initiate conflicts without provocation.

---

## Example Settlements

### Port Hesper

Built around one of Prometheus' oldest surviving orbital elevators, **Port Hesper** has become one of the planet's busiest commercial centers. Merchants from across the Rim regularly exchange manufactured goods, agricultural products, and industrial equipment through its sprawling marketplaces and orbital freight terminals.

Its elected Trade Council has maintained neutrality in numerous regional disputes, making the settlement a favored meeting place for negotiations between otherwise hostile factions.

---

### Ashfall Cooperative

Founded by refugees fleeing a series of volcanic eruptions decades ago, **Ashfall Cooperative** transformed the surrounding basalt plains into productive farmland through extensive geothermal engineering and hydroponics.

The settlement is renowned for welcoming travelers regardless of political affiliation, provided they respect local laws and contribute to the community while visiting.

---

### New Carthage

Established around the remains of an abandoned Corporate House industrial complex, **New Carthage** rebuilt itself into one of the frontier's foremost manufacturing towns.

Independent workshops, machine foundries, and engineering guilds produce everything from agricultural machinery to spacecraft components, earning the settlement a reputation for quality craftsmanship and practical ingenuity.

---

## Relations

Civil Outlanders maintain practical relationships with nearly every major institution operating throughout the frontier.

Many trade extensively with the Corporate Houses while carefully preserving their political independence. Others maintain diplomatic ties with neighboring tribal communities or hire local mercenaries to protect important trade routes.

Although generally distrustful of Pirate Bands, most Civil Outlanders recognize that survival on the Rim often depends upon negotiation as much as military strength.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Civil Outlander settlements represent some of the most prosperous independent communities on Prometheus.

While few rival the resources of the Corporate Houses or the Shattered Imperium, they demonstrate that stable, self-governing societies can thrive even on the edge of civilized space through cooperation, commerce, and mutual defense.
