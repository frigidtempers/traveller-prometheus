Prometheus is home to numerous independent factions differing in culture, political organization, technological development, and territorial influence. No single government exercises authority over the entire planet. Instead, regional powers, independent settlements, tribal communities, pirate organizations, and off-world interests collectively shape the planet's political landscape.

The isolation inherent to rimworlds has encouraged political fragmentation. Communities separated by vast distances, difficult terrain, and infrequent interstellar contact have developed independently over long periods of time. Although trade and diplomacy remain common between neighboring settlements, no centralized authority has successfully unified the planet.

Most inhabitants interact primarily with nearby settlements and regional organizations. Contact with larger interstellar powers is comparatively rare, occurring through trade expeditions, diplomatic missions, scientific surveys, and other occasional visits from off-world spacecraft.

While the factions described below represent the largest documented organizations with a presence on Prometheus, numerous independent settlements, local governments, and temporary alliances exist throughout the planet.

---

## Major Factions

### [[The Empire]]

The Empire maintains a limited but influential presence on Prometheus through noble estates, diplomatic missions, and regional administrators. Although Imperial authority extends beyond the planet, its ability to directly influence local affairs is constrained by the realities of relativistic interstellar travel.

---

### [[Civil Outlanders]]

Civil Outlander settlements represent many of the planet's largest independent communities. They maintain established infrastructure, organized governments, and regular trade with neighboring settlements whenever circumstances permit.

---

### [[Rough Outlanders]]

Rough Outlanders occupy much of Prometheus beyond the larger population centers. Their settlements vary considerably in size and organization, with many emphasizing self-sufficiency and local autonomy.

---

### [[Tribal Communities]]

Tribal communities preserve traditions and social structures that predate many modern settlements. Although technological capabilities differ between communities, tribal societies remain an enduring presence across Prometheus.

---

### [[Pirate Bands]]

Pirate organizations operate throughout remote regions of the planet, targeting trade routes, isolated settlements, and poorly defended facilities. Their composition, leadership, and objectives vary considerably between groups.

---

### [[Corporate Houses]]

The Corporate Houses are powerful interstellar commercial organizations maintaining limited interests on Prometheus. Their activities include trade, resource acquisition, logistics, industrial investment, and scientific expeditions.

Their influence is significant but intermittent, as travel between inhabited systems requires lengthy voyages in cryptosleep.

---

### [[Gas Monkeys]]

The Gas Monkeys are an independent industrial organization specializing in salvage, heavy machinery, fuel production, transportation, and the recovery of abandoned technology.

Although primarily known as mechanics and salvagers, Gas Monkey crews are frequently encountered supporting frontier settlements where technical expertise and industrial equipment are scarce.
