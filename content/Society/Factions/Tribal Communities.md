## Overview

**Tribal Communities** are independent societies organized around kinship, oral tradition, and communal governance rather than centralized industrial institutions. While their cultures differ greatly from one another, most maintain a close relationship with their surrounding environment and preserve traditions passed down over countless generations.

Unlike the Corporate Houses or the Shattered Imperium, Tribal Communities rarely seek territorial expansion beyond what their people require. Each community is culturally distinct, possessing its own customs, beliefs, and methods of governance.

The term *tribal* describes a social structure rather than a level of technological advancement. Although many communities favor traditional craftsmanship and local resources, others incorporate salvaged industrial equipment or recovered technologies without abandoning their cultural identity.

---

## Gentle Tribes

Gentle Tribes are generally welcoming toward outsiders and favor diplomacy over conflict.

These communities often sustain themselves through agriculture, hunting, fishing, and trade with neighboring settlements. Visitors who respect local customs are frequently received as honored guests, and long-term alliances are common.

### Example: Willow Creek

Nestled among Prometheus' southern river valleys, **Willow Creek** has earned a reputation for its skilled herbalists and peaceful relations with neighboring settlements. Seasonal festivals draw merchants from across the region, creating opportunities for trade and cultural exchange that have endured for generations.

---

## Fierce Tribes

Fierce Tribes place great emphasis on martial tradition and the defense of their ancestral lands.

Although capable of diplomacy, these communities expect outsiders to earn their trust through action rather than words. Raids against those who violate tribal territory are viewed not as conquest, but as the fulfillment of a duty to protect the community.

### Example: Red Mesa

Perched atop a series of windswept plateaus, **Red Mesa** is home to a proud warrior society whose members undertake rigorous rites of passage before reaching adulthood. Their scouts are renowned throughout the frontier, and neighboring settlements frequently hire them as guides through the surrounding badlands.

---

## Savage Tribes

Savage Tribes remain deeply isolated from the wider world.

Many inhabit remote jungles, mountain ranges, deserts, or other difficult terrain where contact with outsiders is rare. Centuries of conflict, exploitation, or simple isolation have left these communities deeply distrustful of foreign visitors, and they frequently respond to unexpected incursions with immediate hostility.

While outsiders often regard Savage Tribes as unpredictable, each community possesses its own traditions, laws, and social structures developed over generations of independent existence.

### Example: White Antler

Hidden within the ancient forests of Prometheus' northern continent, **White Antler** has remained largely isolated for centuries. Little is known of its internal customs beyond scattered accounts from explorers fortunate enough to survive accidental encounters with its territory.

---

## Relations

Relations between Tribal Communities and neighboring settlements vary considerably.

Gentle Tribes often maintain active trade with Civil Outlanders, while Fierce Tribes frequently establish defensive alliances based upon mutual respect. Savage Tribes generally avoid sustained contact altogether, preferring to preserve their independence from outside influence.

Many Corporate Houses maintain respectful relationships with tribal leaders when conducting research or resource surveys, recognizing that generations of local knowledge often prove more valuable than sophisticated equipment.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Tribal Communities are found throughout Prometheus, occupying environments ranging from dense forests and fertile river valleys to frozen tundra and arid deserts.

Though their ways of life differ greatly, each community represents a unique cultural tradition shaped by generations of adaptation to the Rimworld. Collectively, they preserve a remarkable diversity of languages, customs, and histories seldom found among the more standardized societies of industrial civilization.
