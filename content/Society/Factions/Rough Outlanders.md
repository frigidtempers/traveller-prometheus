## Overview

**Rough Outlanders** are independent industrial settlements whose cultures have developed under harsh frontier conditions. While possessing many of the same technological capabilities as Civil Outlanders, Rough Outlanders are generally more militarized, insular, and willing to use force to secure their interests.

Each Rough Outlander settlement governs only itself. Alliances between neighboring communities are often temporary and born of necessity, with diplomacy frequently giving way to displays of military strength or economic leverage.

Although outsiders often view them as dangerous, many Rough Outlander settlements simply inhabit regions where cooperation alone has never been enough to ensure survival.

---

## Characteristics

Rough Outlander settlements generally share several common traits.

- Stable, though often authoritarian, governments
- Well-developed industry
- Strong militia or professional security forces
- Armed trade caravans
- Fortified settlements
- Pragmatic diplomacy backed by military strength

While willing to negotiate, Rough Outlanders are considerably more likely than their civil counterparts to resolve disputes through intimidation or force.

## Example Settlements

### Blackrock Hold

Built atop one of Prometheus' richest mineral deposits, **Blackrock Hold** is a heavily fortified mining settlement governed by a council of industrial foremen. Every citizen is expected to contribute to the settlement's defense, and unauthorized entry into its mining territory is treated as an act of trespass.

Despite its uncompromising reputation, Blackrock exports refined metals and industrial equipment throughout the surrounding region.

---

### Crow's End

Situated along a strategic mountain pass, **Crow's End** controls one of the few reliable overland trade routes through the northern highlands.

The settlement finances its defenses through tariffs levied on commercial caravans. While travelers often complain about its expensive tolls and strict regulations, few dispute the effectiveness of its patrols in keeping the roads free from raiders.

---

### Rustpoint

Originally established around the remains of a pre-collapse industrial complex, **Rustpoint** has become a thriving center for heavy industry and weapons manufacturing.

Its workshops produce everything from agricultural machinery to military hardware, attracting traders, mercenaries, and independent prospectors from across Prometheus. The settlement's leadership maintains a policy of armed neutrality, conducting business with nearly anyone willing to respect its sovereignty.

---

## Relations

Rough Outlanders frequently maintain complicated relationships with neighboring settlements.

Civil Outlanders often regard them as unnecessarily aggressive, while Rough Outlanders view their more diplomatic counterparts as naive. Despite these differences, trade between the two remains common, particularly where each possesses resources the other cannot easily obtain.

Relations with Pirate Bands are typically hostile, though isolated settlements have occasionally resorted to temporary arrangements when circumstances demand.

Corporate Houses generally view Rough Outlanders as reliable commercial partners provided contractual obligations are honored, while the Shattered Imperium tends to respect their emphasis on discipline, self-reliance, and local authority.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Rough Outlander settlements are common throughout Prometheus' frontier regions.

Many occupy strategic mountain passes, mining districts, industrial ruins, or defensible natural terrain where security remains a constant concern. Although often perceived as unfriendly by newcomers, these communities have earned reputations for resilience, independence, and an unwavering determination to protect what they have built.
