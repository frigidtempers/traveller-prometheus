## Overview

The **Gas Monkeys** are an independent industrial cooperative specializing in the extraction, refinement, and transportation of fuel throughout the frontier. Originally formed by refinery workers, fuel haulers, orbital mechanics, and independent miners, the organization has grown into one of the largest non-government fuel suppliers operating beyond the Core Worlds.

Although lacking the resources of the Corporate Houses, the Gas Monkeys maintain an extensive network of refineries, fuel depots, orbital tank farms, and tanker vessels that support thousands of independent settlements. Their willingness to operate where larger organizations consider business unprofitable has earned them a reputation as one of the Rim's most dependable industrial organizations.

---

## History

The Gas Monkeys originated as a loose association of refinery workers and independent fuel haulers operating beyond established commercial networks.

Unable to compete directly with the logistical capabilities of the Corporate Houses, numerous small fuel producers gradually formed cooperative agreements that standardized equipment, pricing, maintenance procedures, and mutual assistance. Over generations these agreements evolved into the organization now recognized throughout the frontier as the Gas Monkeys.

While individual member companies remain independently owned, they collectively maintain one of the largest fuel distribution networks outside Corporate House control.

---

## Organization

The Gas Monkeys operate as a federation of independently owned refineries, extraction facilities, transport companies, and maintenance crews.

Major organizational decisions are made through elected representatives rather than hereditary leadership or corporate appointment. Individual members retain considerable operational independence provided they adhere to cooperative standards regarding fuel quality, safety, and mutual assistance.

This decentralized structure has allowed the organization to remain resilient despite operating across worlds separated by years of interstellar travel.

---

## Culture

The Gas Monkeys value practicality, self-reliance, and mutual support above nearly everything else.

Their members generally believe that no settlement should be abandoned simply because supplying it is inconvenient or unprofitable. This philosophy has made them particularly popular among frontier colonies that frequently receive little attention from larger commercial organizations.

Among cooperative members, a common saying persists:

> *"No fuel. No future."*

---

## Operations

The cooperative specializes in every stage of industrial fuel production and distribution.

Its affiliated organizations operate drilling platforms, chemfuel refineries, orbital fuel depots, tanker fleets, emergency resupply vessels, and maintenance facilities throughout much of the frontier. Many settlements rely upon the Gas Monkeys as their primary supplier of reactor fuel, industrial chemicals, and emergency power reserves.

Although they rarely manufacture spacecraft themselves, Gas Monkey crews possess a well-earned reputation for keeping aging engines and neglected reactors operational under extremely difficult conditions.

---

## Relations

The Gas Monkeys maintain generally positive relationships with independent settlements and most frontier communities.

Relations with the Corporate Houses are cooperative but competitive. House Amora frequently contracts Gas Monkey facilities to supplement regional fuel production, while House Belmar manufactures much of the heavy equipment utilized throughout the cooperative's industrial network.

The Shattered Imperium recognizes the cooperative as an important commercial partner, though its decentralized governance occasionally complicates formal diplomatic negotiations.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

The Gas Monkeys maintain several fuel depots and refining operations throughout Prometheus.

Their crews are frequently encountered wherever generators, industrial machinery, spacecraft, or mining operations require reliable fuel supplies. Among isolated settlements, the arrival of a Gas Monkey tanker is often regarded with the same relief as the arrival of food or medicine, particularly during the planet's harsh seasonal shortages.
