## Overview

The **Shattered Imperium** is an ultratech interstellar polity descended from the former **Empire of Sophiamunda**. Although the Sophian Empire no longer exists, its surviving noble houses, military institutions, and traditions continue to shape one of the most influential civilizations in known space.

Despite the loss of much of its former territory, the Imperium remains a major political and military power. Its advanced technology, disciplined military, and enduring aristocracy ensure that Imperial authority is respected far beyond the borders of its directly administered worlds.

---

## History

The modern Imperium traces its origins to the collapse of the **Empire of Sophiamunda**, once among humanity's greatest civilizations.

The exact circumstances surrounding Sophiamunda's downfall remain poorly documented outside Imperial archives. Contemporary records agree only that a catastrophe shattered Sophian society, forcing its surviving leadership, military, and civilian population to abandon their homeworld. Over the centuries that followed, these refugees reorganized into the polity now known as the **Shattered Imperium**, preserving much of the Empire's governmental structure, customs, and technological knowledge. 

---

## Government

The Imperium is governed through a hereditary feudal hierarchy headed by an Emperor, though day-to-day authority is exercised by regional Stellarchs, planetary nobles, and lesser aristocrats.

Titles carry significant legal, political, and military authority. Advancement within Imperial society is largely determined through noble appointment, military distinction, or extraordinary service to the Crown.

---

## Society

Imperial society places great importance on honor, etiquette, and social order.

Centuries of tradition have produced an elaborate system of noble titles, ceremonial customs, and expectations governing public conduct. While technological advancement remains central to Imperial civilization, its culture deliberately preserves institutions that predate humanity's expansion across the stars.

---

## Military

The Imperial military fields some of the most technologically advanced forces known to exist, combining ultratech equipment, persona weaponry, psychic specialists, and highly trained professional soldiers.

Rather than relying upon overwhelming numbers, Imperial doctrine emphasizes disciplined formations, superior technology, and decisive application of force.

---

## Foreign Relations

The Imperium maintains diplomatic and commercial relations with a wide variety of independent worlds, Corporate Houses, and regional governments.

Although its influence extends across numerous star systems, the realities of interstellar travel require significant autonomy among local authorities. Imperial policy therefore favors alliances, treaties, and mutual obligation over direct centralized administration.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

Imperial influence on Prometheus remains limited.

The Imperium maintains only a small permanent presence on the planet, primarily through diplomatic representatives, noble expeditions, and scientific missions. Even so, Imperial titles, customs, and authority continue to carry considerable prestige among many of Prometheus' settlements.
