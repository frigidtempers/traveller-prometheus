## Overview

The **Corporate Houses** are hereditary commercial conglomerates that control much of humanity's interstellar economy. Each House oversees a vast network of subsidiary corporations, regional enterprises, colonial administrations, and commercial partnerships operating across numerous inhabited systems.

Although frequently compared to the feudal institutions of the Shattered Imperium, the Corporate Houses derive their authority from ownership rather than noble title. Leadership is inherited through corporate succession, with each generation assuming stewardship of commercial empires that often predate the worlds upon which they now operate.

Individual corporations are routinely founded, merged, dissolved, or reorganized to meet changing economic demands. The Houses themselves, however, remain remarkably stable, preserving centuries of accumulated wealth, institutional knowledge, and commercial influence.

## Major Corporate Houses

### [[House Helix]]

**Flagship Corporation:** *NuTerra Corp.*

House Helix dominates the agricultural sector through an extensive portfolio of companies specializing in food production, terraforming, ecological engineering, and colonial sustainability.

---

### [[House Belmar]]

**Flagship Corporation:** *InnoTech Industries*

House Belmar oversees manufacturing, industrial automation, heavy machinery, robotics, and infrastructure development across much of known space.

---

### [[House Gol]]

**Flagship Corporation:** *Seraph Entertainment*

House Gol controls a diverse collection of media, communications, hospitality, and luxury brands whose cultural influence extends well beyond the markets they serve.

---

### [[House Amora]]

**Flagship Corporation:** *Dawn Star United*

House Amora maintains one of humanity's largest commercial transportation networks, coordinating interstellar logistics, freight, passenger travel, and colonial supply chains through hundreds of affiliated enterprises.

---

### [[House Xiaomu]]

**Flagship Corporation:** *Meditech*

House Xiaomu directs a vast biomedical network encompassing hospitals, pharmaceutical manufacturers, prosthetics laboratories, xenogenetics firms, and advanced medical research organizations.

## Relations with the Shattered Imperium

The Corporate Houses and the Shattered Imperium maintain a relationship defined by mutual necessity.

Imperial worlds depend upon House industry, logistics, and manufacturing, while the Houses benefit from the stability, legal institutions, and political legitimacy provided by Imperial governance. Despite frequent commercial and diplomatic disputes, both recognize that long-term cooperation is preferable to open conflict.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

All five major Corporate Houses maintain interests on Prometheus.

Most settlements encounter the Houses not through their governing families, but through the countless subsidiary corporations operating mines, hospitals, farms, factories, research stations, and transportation networks across the planet. While the names of these local enterprises vary, their ownership ultimately traces back to one of the great Corporate Houses.
