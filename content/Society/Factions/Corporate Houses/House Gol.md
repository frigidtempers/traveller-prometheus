## Overview

**House Gol** is one of the five major Corporate Houses and the largest commercial authority within the fields of media, communications, entertainment, hospitality, and consumer culture. Through an immense network of publishers, broadcasters, production studios, luxury brands, educational services, and communications companies, House Gol influences how much of humanity experiences information, art, and recreation.

Unlike the industrial focus of House Belmar or the logistical networks maintained by House Amora, House Gol's influence is measured less through infrastructure than through culture. Its subsidiaries reach billions of people across countless worlds, shaping everything from popular entertainment and fashion to advertising and public communications.

---

## History

House Gol originated as a collection of publishing houses and communications firms serving humanity's early interstellar colonies. As civilization expanded, these enterprises gradually diversified into nearly every aspect of commercial media, eventually consolidating under a single hereditary commercial dynasty.

Over centuries, House Gol expanded far beyond traditional entertainment. Hospitality, tourism, luxury goods, advertising, education, consumer electronics, and planetary communications all became integral parts of its commercial portfolio, establishing the House as one of humanity's foremost cultural institutions.

---

## Organization

House Gol oversees hundreds of independently managed corporations operating across the communications and consumer sectors.

Regional subsidiaries maintain considerable creative and commercial independence while adhering to House standards regarding intellectual property, branding, and long-term strategic development. This decentralized structure allows local companies to reflect the cultures of individual worlds while remaining connected to a larger commercial network.

---

## Culture

House Gol believes that culture is one of civilization's most enduring achievements.

Employees are encouraged to view their work not simply as entertainment or commerce, but as the preservation and exchange of human experiences across the immense distances separating inhabited worlds. Whether producing educational material for frontier settlements or financing major artistic productions on glitterworlds, the House regards communication as a form of infrastructure every bit as essential as transportation or manufacturing.

Among House personnel, a common saying persists:

> *"Civilizations are remembered by the stories they leave behind."*

---

## Notable Holdings

The following organizations represent only a small portion of House Gol's commercial portfolio.

- **Seraph Entertainment** — Film, music, literature, and interactive media.
- **Seventh Heaven Broadcasting Group** — Interstellar news and public communications.
- **Aurora Hospitality** — Hotels, resorts, restaurants, and tourism.
- **Crown & Quill Publishing** — Educational materials, historical archives, and commercial publishing.
- **Halo Luxury Collective** — Fashion, jewelry, consumer goods, and premium lifestyle brands.

---

## Relations

House Gol maintains extensive commercial relationships with every major Corporate House and the Shattered Imperium.

Its communications networks, educational services, and media organizations operate on worlds governed by a wide variety of political systems. While generally avoiding direct political involvement, the House frequently serves as an intermediary for public information campaigns, cultural initiatives, and commercial advertising.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

House Gol maintains a comparatively modest physical presence on Prometheus, though its influence remains widespread.

Communications infrastructure, educational institutions, hospitality businesses, and locally operated media organizations frequently operate under one of House Gol's affiliated corporations. For many settlements, Gol-owned subsidiaries provide the primary connection to news, entertainment, and cultural developments originating beyond the Rimworld.
