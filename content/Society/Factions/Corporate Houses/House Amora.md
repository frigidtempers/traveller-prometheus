## Overview

**House Amora** is one of the five major Corporate Houses and the dominant force in interstellar logistics, transportation, and colonial infrastructure. Through an extensive network of shipping companies, freight carriers, orbital construction firms, and colonial support organizations, House Amora facilitates the movement of people, resources, and information across inhabited space.

Although most individuals never encounter the House directly, countless commercial enterprises ultimately operate under its ownership. From orbital elevators and cargo terminals to passenger liners and frontier supply depots, House Amora's influence extends to nearly every stage of interstellar commerce.

---

## History

House Amora traces its origins to a consortium of merchant fleets established during humanity's early interstellar expansion. As colonies multiplied across the stars, independent shipping concerns gradually consolidated under shared ownership, forming a commercial dynasty whose influence expanded alongside civilization itself.

Over centuries, House Amora evolved beyond transportation alone. Recognizing that successful colonies required more than ships, the House invested heavily in orbital infrastructure, logistics, warehousing, customs administration, and colonial support services. Today, its commercial empire spans industries that make long-distance settlement economically viable.

---

## Organization

Unlike conventional corporations, House Amora functions as a hereditary commercial institution.

Day-to-day operations are carried out by hundreds of subsidiary corporations, each specializing in a particular sector of transportation or infrastructure. These companies maintain considerable operational independence while ultimately remaining accountable to House leadership through a complex network of ownership, contractual obligations, and long-standing commercial traditions.

This decentralized structure allows House Amora to continue operating effectively despite the immense distances separating inhabited systems.

---

## Culture

House Amora places exceptional value on reliability, punctuality, and continuity.

Employees are expected to view logistics as more than transportation alone. Every shipment represents a commitment between worlds, and every successful delivery reinforces the trust upon which interstellar civilization depends.

Among House personnel, a common saying persists:

> *"A delayed shipment is a broken promise."*

---

## Notable Holdings

The following organizations represent only a small portion of House Amora's commercial portfolio.

- **Dawn Star United** — Interstellar freight and passenger transportation.
- **Horizon Orbital Works** — Construction of orbital stations, docks, and shipyards.
- **Meridian Colonial Services** — Colonial infrastructure, utilities, and settlement development.
- **Atlas Freight Exchange** — Cargo brokerage and commercial logistics.
- **Wayfinder Logistics** — Astrogation, navigation services, and shipping coordination.

---

## Relations

House Amora maintains commercial relationships with nearly every major political and economic institution in known space.

The Shattered Imperium relies upon Amora shipping networks to sustain many frontier holdings, while the remaining Corporate Houses depend upon its logistics infrastructure to transport manufactured goods, agricultural products, pharmaceuticals, and industrial equipment between worlds.

Despite its extensive influence, House Amora rarely becomes directly involved in political disputes, preferring commercial neutrality whenever practical.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

House Amora maintains a substantial presence throughout Prometheus.

Its subsidiaries operate orbital cargo facilities, shuttle terminals, warehousing complexes, and commercial infrastructure supporting both local settlements and off-world trade. Many colonists arriving on Prometheus first encounter House Amora through **Dawn Star United**, whose vessels remain the primary means of civilian transportation to and from the Rimworld.

Although House executives rarely visit the frontier personally, the House's logistical network serves as one of the principal connections between Prometheus and the wider human diaspora.
