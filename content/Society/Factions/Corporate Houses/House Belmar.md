## Overview

**House Belmar** is one of the five major Corporate Houses and the foremost authority in industrial manufacturing throughout known space. Through an immense network of foundries, fabrication plants, robotics firms, construction companies, and engineering contractors, the House produces much of the machinery and infrastructure upon which interstellar civilization depends.

From orbital shipyards to frontier workshops, Belmar-built equipment is regarded as the industry standard. While the names of individual manufacturers vary from world to world, many ultimately trace their ownership to House Belmar.

---

## History

House Belmar emerged during humanity's expansion into the stars as countless regional manufacturers consolidated under a common commercial dynasty. As new colonies demanded machinery capable of operating in hostile environments, the House expanded beyond traditional manufacturing into heavy industry, industrial automation, and planetary infrastructure.

Over centuries, its portfolio grew to encompass nearly every stage of industrial production, from raw material processing to the construction of complete colonial settlements.

---

## Organization

House Belmar governs an extensive network of independently managed corporations specializing in manufacturing, engineering, mining equipment, robotics, construction, and industrial services.

Individual companies maintain their own leadership, branding, and regional operations while benefiting from House-owned patents, production standards, and supply networks. This decentralized structure allows Belmar industries to remain resilient despite the vast distances separating inhabited worlds.

---

## Culture

House Belmar values precision, reliability, and continual improvement.

Employees are encouraged to view every product as an extension of the House's reputation. Whether constructing orbital shipyards or manufacturing simple hand tools, quality is considered inseparable from professional integrity.

Among Belmar engineers, a common saying persists:

> *"If it bears our mark, it must endure."*

---

## Notable Holdings

The following organizations represent only a small portion of House Belmar's industrial portfolio.

- **InnoTech Industries** — Industrial manufacturing and heavy engineering.
- **Atlas Forge Works** — Heavy machinery and mining equipment.
- **Titan Fabrication Group** — Structural steel and modular construction.
- **Forge Dynamics** — Industrial robotics and automated production systems.
- **Keystone Industrial Solutions** — Planetary infrastructure and public works.

---

## Relations

House Belmar maintains close commercial relationships with both the Shattered Imperium and the other Corporate Houses.

House Amora depends upon Belmar-built vessels, cargo handling equipment, and orbital infrastructure. House Helix utilizes Belmar agricultural machinery throughout its colonial holdings, while House Xiaomu relies upon precision manufacturing for advanced medical equipment.

Competition between Houses is common, though Belmar's role as a supplier often places it in the position of collaborator rather than direct rival.

---

## Presence on Prometheus

House Belmar maintains extensive industrial operations across Prometheus.

Mining settlements, machine shops, fabrication plants, and construction projects throughout the Rimworld frequently utilize Belmar-designed equipment or are operated by one of the House's affiliated corporations. Many frontier settlements rely upon Belmar machinery that has remained in continuous operation for generations, supported through a vast network of replacement parts and regional service providers.
